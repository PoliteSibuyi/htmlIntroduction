Problem
Sentiment analysis is a branch of machine learning that detects the emotions mood, or
feelings expressed by the author of a written document. In its simplest form, the
sentiment analysis algorithms calculate the frequency of words that express emotions in
the text.
Emotions from text can be positive [happy/good, cool], negative [sad, bad] or neutral.
Every spoken language has a list of words that communicate positive or negative
sentiment:
i.e. English
Positive sentiment: Exciting, adorable, amazing, good, interesting, good etc.
Negative sentiment: Disappointing, sad, shame, suffering, bad.
In simplest terms, a document's sentiment is determined by the comparing total number
of the negative and total number of positive words. If the total number of positive is
greater than the total number of negative words then the document is classified as
having a positive sentiment and vice versa.
